Admission Process

Delhi Technological University (DTU) welcomes aspiring students to apply for various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. We provide a holistic environment for learning and professional growth.

Steps for Admission

1

Online Registration

Visit the DTU admission portal and fill out the online registration form with accurate details.

2

Eligibility Check

Ensure that you meet the eligibility criteria for the program you are applying for.

3

Entrance Examination

Appear for the entrance exam, which will assess your knowledge and skills in the respective subject areas.

4

Document Verification

Provide your necessary documents for verification to confirm your eligibility and academic credentials.

5

Final Selection

If selected, you will be invited for counseling and seat allocation.
